FOUNDER NOTE

A simple
observation.

Midnight Space was founded by Jordan Van Der Sloten in 2026 on a simple observation: the people moving the GCC space sector forward — agencies, investors, operators, founders — were each rebuilding the same funding spreadsheet from scratch.

We're small, self-funded, and pre-revenue by choice. The platform is a working prototype. Coverage is real but partial. We'd rather ship one honest tool than a perfect roadmap.

We work closely with a handful of pilot partners. Their feedback shapes what we build next, in that order.

PRINCIPLES

How we
actually work.

Three small commitments that shape every decision — from what we ship to who we work with.

01 — Honesty

No vapourware.

If a module isn't live, we say it's planned. If coverage stops at the GCC, we say so. The roadmap is honest, even when it's slow.

02 — Independence

No house view.

We're self-funded and unaffiliated. No agency or fund owns a slice of the data. Coverage decisions are editorial, not commercial.

03 — Service

Few partners, deep.

A handful of pilot partners — not a sales funnel. Their feedback compounds into the platform, in their priority order.
