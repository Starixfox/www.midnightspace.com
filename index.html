Platform · 01

An honest platform.
Built in orbit.

Midnight Space is a working prototype, not a brochure. The Saudi Space Opportunity Hub is live with real data on funding calls, grants, accelerators, and public programs across the region.

We're focused on the GCC first because that's where the questions are most concentrated and the answers are least centralised. From there, we extend coverage outward — Europe, North America, Asia, and emerging markets.

We're early. We're looking for pilot partners who want to shape what gets built.

We started Midnight Space because the people who need this data — agencies, investors, operators — were each rebuilding the same spreadsheet from scratch. One honest source, kept current, beats ten outdated ones. Jordan Van Der Sloten  ·  Founder, Midnight Space

About · 03

Founded in 2026.
Independent by design.

STUDIO / OBJ-01NIGHT-SIDE EXPOSURE

Midnight Space was founded by Jordan Van Der Sloten in 2026 on a simple observation: the people moving the GCC space sector forward — agencies, investors, operators, founders — were each rebuilding the same funding spreadsheet from scratch.

We're small, self-funded, and pre-revenue by choice. The platform is a working prototype. Coverage is real but partial. We'd rather ship one honest tool than a perfect roadmap.

We work closely with a handful of pilot partners. Their feedback shapes what we build next, in that order.
