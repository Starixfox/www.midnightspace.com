COVERAGE

A Region
in Motion.

Saudi Arabia and the GCC are quietly running one of the most active space-funding cycles in the world — grants, accelerators, sovereign programs, and public tenders, scattered across dozens of agencies and ministries.

Midnight Space pulls these into a single, current, honest source. From there we extend outward: Europe, North America, Asia, and the emerging markets where the next operators are coming from.
